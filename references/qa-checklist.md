# Presentation QA Checklist

Execute this checklist after generating both formats and repeat it after corrections.

## Source integrity

- Original files remain unchanged unless in-place editing was explicitly requested.
- Slide/page count and intentional content changes are documented.
- No source claims, citations, notes, charts, or required brand elements were silently dropped.

## Narrative

- Audience, purpose, and desired outcome are evident.
- Action titles are supported by the slide content.
- The title-only story is coherent and ordered.
- Each slide has one clear communicative purpose.
- Opening establishes relevance and closing provides synthesis or action.

## Visual system

- Aspect ratio, margins, grid, typography, colors, spacing, imagery, charts, and recurring components are consistent.
- Visual rhythm varies deliberately without appearing random.
- No slide is an unexplained stylistic outlier.
- Decorative elements do not compete with evidence.

## Full-resolution inspection

- No overflow, overlap, clipping, accidental crop, broken glyph, missing character, or unexpected font fallback.
- Body text, captions, sources, labels, and notes are legible.
- Images are sufficiently sharp at output size.
- Charts have honest scales, explicit units, readable labels, and a clear emphasis.
- Tables fit, align, and prioritize the intended comparison.
- Citations are present and readable.

## Montage inspection

- Density and whitespace are balanced across the sequence.
- Repeated layouts do not become monotonous.
- Section transitions and key moments are visually apparent.
- No slide is obviously too empty, too dense, too dark, too bright, or misaligned relative to its neighbors.

## Accessibility

- Text and essential marks have sufficient contrast.
- Meaning is not encoded by color alone.
- Typography is readable at presentation distance.
- Reading order and labels are unambiguous.

## Artifact checks

- PPTX opens successfully and expected text, shapes, charts, and images remain editable.
- PDF opens successfully and every page renders.
- PPTX slides and PDF pages match in count, order, content, and appearance.
- Speaker notes are included when required and contain no generation instructions.
- Filenames are clear and final files contain no placeholders, debug marks, comments, or temporary assets.

## Delivery gate

Do not deliver until every applicable check passes or the user has been told exactly which limitation remains and why it could not be corrected.
